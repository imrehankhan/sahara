Who we are?

We, team Sahara, aim to achieve food security, end hunger, and minimize food wastage. We bring spark in the eyes of the needy who find it difficult to feed their family on a regular basis and are struggling to fight starvation. To know more about the crisis click here

CRISIS

According to United Nations, more than 600 million people worldwide are projected to face hunger by 2030. 1 in 3 people worldwide struggle with serving their family on a regular basis.

HELPING HAND
